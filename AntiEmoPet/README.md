# Ideation
To help fight with SAD, light affected emotional fluctuations and seasonal low mood. Target to provide personalised mood-boosting recommendations — from quick outdoor tasks on sunny days to music, videos, or light-therapy content on dark or rainy days — helping users maintain emotional balance throughout the winter.
这款应用旨在帮助用户应对季节性情绪低落（SAD）以及因光照变化带来的情绪波动。通过个性化的情绪提升建议，包括晴天出门散步或者买咖啡一类的小任务，阴雨天在室内可进行的音乐、视频推荐或者光疗，帮助用户在漫长的冬季保持情绪平衡与活力。
